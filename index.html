Privacy Policy for SpeechNova

Effective Date: 20/02/2026

SpeechNova (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our speech-to-speech translation services.

Information We Collect

  • Microphone Access: The App uses your device’s microphone to capture speech input for translation. This audio is processed locally on your device and is not stored or transmitted to external servers.
  • Advertising ID: If you use the free version of the App, banner ads may be displayed. These ads are provided through third-party SDKs (such as Google Mobile Ads SDK), which may use your device’s Advertising ID for ad personalization and performance measurement.
  • No Personal Accounts: The App does not require you to create an account or provide personal information such as name, email, or phone number.

How We Use Information

  • Translation Functionality: Speech input is used solely to provide real-time translation.
  • Advertising: Advertising ID may be used by third-party SDKs to deliver relevant ads and measure ad performance.
  • Analytics (if applicable): Anonymous usage data may be collected by third-party SDKs to improve app performance and reliability.

Data Sharing

  • We do not share your speech data or personal information with any third parties.
  • Third-party advertising SDKs may collect and use Advertising ID in accordance with their own privacy policies. For details, please review Google’s Privacy Policy.

Security

  • All translations are processed offline on your device.
  • No speech data is uploaded to the cloud.
  • We minimize permissions: only microphone access is required for functionality.

Children’s Privacy

SpeechNova is suitable for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated effective date.
